About Me

I love telling stories with data and uncovering insights from them. I'm based in Salem, Oregon. I got into Data Analytics & Science full-time in 2022 after completing the University of Oregon Data Analytics Bootcamp. During the Bootcamp we completed projects using frameworks in Python such as Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Scikit Learn, NLTK, TextBlob and Visualization tools like Tableau, Plotly & PowerBI. I am currently collaborating on more personal projects that are focused on enriching lives and adding value.

When I am not analysing datasets or writing code, I read about new trends in the data industry, I love to coach soccer, and spend time with friends and family.
